NEXT Engineering Consultants founded by Eng. Nazih and established in 1994 in Dubai, UAE. The company have expanded it's branches world-wide as a consultant for unlimited floors building in different provisions such as in UAE-Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah and Fujairah. As well as in Lebanon-Beirut, Canada-Ottawa, Iraq-Baghdad and Oman-Muscut.

The ultimate goal of Eng. Nazih was to always look forward for the next step. His consultancy has played major role in the development of the designs within the world along with all across the UAE and has adopted the comprehensive development philosophy. Therefore,his consultancy continuously seek to keep pace in terms of the development through his achievements of engineering services required by high standards and quality to match the high standards within the country and through the client's needs

Next Engineering Consultants believes that faithful efforts and dedicated work is what the company emphasize in the next step, in order to achieve a distinctive local, regional and international position
